Que-2. What you think is best thing about the web?

The web's universality and accessibility are two of its best features. Any device with an internet connection may access the web, enabling individuals all over the world to exchange and access knowledge, resources, and ideas.

The web's capacity to link individuals and enable communication and collaboration is yet another fantastic feature. No matter where they are in the world, people can quickly interact and communicate with one another via social media, forums, email, and other web-based tools.

Additionally, e-commerce, online shopping, and business are made feasible by the web, allowing people to buy and sell goods and services throughout the globe.

The web is a priceless instrument for education and personal development since it provides a wide range of information and resources, including news, entertainment, knowledge, and education.
